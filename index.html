What have we done with your help?

Whatever resources you contibuted, have had a tremendous positive impact on lives of thousands of kids. The funds have been utilised to build better schools, provide medical facilities, provide food to the hungry and intallation of water purification systems across various towns in India.

As our efforts keep rising, so does the need for funds to continue our operations. To reach as many people as possible, a large team is needed. Our team comprises of people who have left their full-time jobs to work for us and support a noble cause, hence lack of funds sometimes prove to be a hindering cause, nonetheless, what keeps these amazing volunteers happy is the feeling of satisfaction which they get after putting a smile on a face.

No matter what the circumstances are, we will continue to move forward and change lives for better.
